Our Process

Tea Quality & Process

From tea gardens to your cup - we ensure the highest quality at every step

1. Sustainable Sourcing

We directly source our fresh tea leaves India from trusted tea gardens in Uttarakhand. Our farmers follow traditional, chemical-free farming practices to ensure pure, natural tea leaves.

2. Quality Testing

Every batch of premium Indian tea undergoes rigorous quality testing. We check for purity, moisture content, and flavor profile to ensure only the best tea leaves make it to packaging.

3. Fresh Packaging

Our natural tea brand India products are packed in food-grade, airtight packaging to preserve the strong aroma and freshness. We offer both 50g and 250g packs for retail and wholesale.

4. Timely Delivery

As a reliable tea supplier for retailers, we ensure pan-India delivery. Our efficient logistics network ensures that retailers and distributors receive fresh stock on time, every time.
